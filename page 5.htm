The Strands That we Offer:


  
  
S.T.E.M. 
Science, Technology, Engineering, 
        and Mathematics strand. Through the STEM strand, 
        are exposed to complex  mathematical and science 
        theories and concepts.
G.A.S. 
General Academic Strand takes a general 
        approach to prepare students for university life. 
        Compared to other strands, students can expect to tackle 
        different disciplines.
  
  
  
H.U.M.S.S. 
The Humanities and Social Sciences covers topics 
            in the liberal arts, training students to think, write, 
            and speak about various humanistic and societal concerns.
I.C.T/E.I.M. 
Information Communication and Technology 
            seek to teach students concepts and skills in 
            information technology.

            Electrical Installation and Maintenance hone the skills of 
            the students to repair and installation of refrigeration 
            and air-conditioning servicing, transmission line 
            installation and maintenance, electrical installation 
            and maintenance.
